# 🌌 From Bits to Cosmic Intelligence

## 🚀 Overview
A comprehensive, structured roadmap that traces the evolution of computation—from primitive calculation tools to the theoretical limits of intelligence at a cosmic scale.

This repository is not just a learning guide. It is a **multi-layered model of intelligence evolution**, connecting:

> Bits → Logic → Systems → AI → Intelligence → Universe

---

# 🧠 Core Philosophy

- Intelligence is **built layer by layer**
- Every advanced system depends on lower-level abstractions
- Computation is the **foundation of all intelligence**
